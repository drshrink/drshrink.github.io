Welcome to CNDL

Brain is a network which has both wired and wireless system. It means that brain regions are connected both anatomically and functionally.

Various brain disorders (e.g., schizophrenia, ADHD, Autism) are related with the developmental abnormalities and also have abnormal connectivity in their brain network.

We have explored anatomo-functional network in a same person using multimodal neuroimaging (fMRI, MEG, DTI) technique in brain developmental disorders and have also studied the environmental effect (e.g., childhood maltreatment) on brain development.

We have been focusing on both the establishment of the individualized model of network abnormalities of brain developmental disorder and the effect of the environment on brain network. And we have been figuring out how/what both pharmacological and non-pharmacological treatment work on brain network.

We believe our multimodal and multi-aspect approach can be applied both to understand normal/abnormal brain behavior and can help to develop the individualized treatment.
